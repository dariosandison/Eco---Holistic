## Email 3 — No‑spend wins first (Day 2)
**Subject:** The no‑spend steps that actually matter

Before buying anything, do one of these:

**Air**
- 5–10 minutes of window opening twice/day  
- run bathroom/kitchen extraction after moisture/cooking

**Fragrance‑free**
- reduce detergent dose; add an extra rinse for bedding

**Sleep**
- daylight soon after waking (even cloudy UK days count)
