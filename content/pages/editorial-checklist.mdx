# Editorial Checklist

## Accuracy
- Claims backed by primary sources; no overreach.
- Dates, prices, and model names verified.

## Reader Value
- Intro states the outcome the reader gets.
- Pros/cons reflect real trade-offs; no fluff.

## Links & Disclosures
- Affiliate links use `/go/*` and `rel="nofollow sponsored"`.
- Alternatives are included when relevant.

## UX
- Descriptive H1/H2s; scannable bullets.
- Tables have plain-language labels.

## Updates
- Timestamp significant edits.
- Note when recommendations change and why.
