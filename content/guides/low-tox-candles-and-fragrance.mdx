# Low‑Tox Candles & Home Fragrance

Scent without the headache.

## Safer Choices
- **Beeswax/soy/rapeseed** candles with cotton wicks.
- **Essential‑oil** diffusers (short sessions).

## Ventilate
- Crack a window after burning; trim wicks.
