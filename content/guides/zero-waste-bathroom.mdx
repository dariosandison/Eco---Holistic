# Zero‑Waste Bathroom

Reduce plastic, keep it practical.

## Swaps
- **Bar soap** and **shampoo bars**.
- **Safety razor** with recyclable blades.
- **Refill** handwash and cleaners.

## Habits
- Use every last drop; store spares cool and dry.
