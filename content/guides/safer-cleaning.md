## Quick recipes (always spot-test)
- **All-purpose:** 1 tbsp castile + 500 ml warm water.  
- **Glass:** 1:1 water:vinegar, microfibre cloth.  
- **Bathroom spray:** 1 tsp castile + 500 ml water; rinse surfaces.
