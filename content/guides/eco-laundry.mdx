> **TL;DR:** Use a **liquid concentrate** or **powder** (not pods), wash cold, and catch microfibres with a wash bag or filter.

## What to buy

- **Detergent:** powder or liquid concentrate with short ingredient lists.

- **Stain stick:** simple surfactants + enzymes.

- **Microfibre capture:** wash bag or in‑line filter.
