# Winter Humidity Guide

Comfort without condensation.

## Targets
- Keep indoor **RH 40–50%**.
- If windows fog, lower humidifier output and ventilate.

## Tips
- Dry clothes outside room air when possible.
- Dehumidify bathrooms after showers.
