# Low‑Tox Kitchen Setup

Cookware and storage you’ll use for years.

## Core Picks
- **Stainless steel** pots, **cast iron** skillet.
- **Wood or silicone** utensils.
- **Glass** or **steel** storage.

## Avoid
- Scratched non‑stick; replace or switch to ceramic/steel/iron.
- Plastic touching high heat.
