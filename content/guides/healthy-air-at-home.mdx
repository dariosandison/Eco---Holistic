> **TL;DR:** Ventilate during and after high-moisture tasks, vacuum with a HEPA filter weekly, and use low-VOC products.

## Quick wins

- **Ventilation:** 10–20 minutes after shower/cooking.
- **Dust control:** vacuum with **HEPA** once a week.
- **Low-VOC products:** avoid heavy fragrance and aerosols.

## Helpful tools

- **HEPA vacuum** — sealed system.
- **Humidity monitor** — aim for **40–60% RH**.
- **Dehumidifier** if RH stays high.
