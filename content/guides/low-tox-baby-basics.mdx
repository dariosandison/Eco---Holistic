# Low‑Tox Baby Basics

Less stuff, better materials.

## Buy Once
- **Cot mattress** with safe FR strategy.
- **Glass** bottles or high‑quality PP.
- **Fragrance‑free** wipes and creams.

## Skip
- Scented room sprays, wipe warmers, bulky plastic toys.
