> **TL;DR:** Start with a **bokashi bin** for meat/dairy scraps and a small **worm bin** or council green waste for the rest.

## Choose a method

- **Bokashi** — ferments everything; finish in soil or council bin.  
- **Worm bin** — efficient for veg/coffee; needs airflow and carbon.
