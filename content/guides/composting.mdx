# Composting Basics

Turn scraps into soil—flat or garden.

## Start
- Choose **bokashi**, **worm bin**, or **cold heap**.
- Balance **greens** (wet) and **browns** (dry).

## Easy Wins
- Chop scraps small; avoid big bones and whole corn cobs.
- Keep it **moist but not soggy**.
