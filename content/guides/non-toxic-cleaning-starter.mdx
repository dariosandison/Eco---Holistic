# Non‑Toxic Cleaning Starter

A small kit handles 90% of jobs.

## Core Kit
- **All‑purpose**: diluted surfactant cleaner.
- **Degreaser**: washing‑up liquid + hot water.
- **Abrasive**: baking soda paste.
- **Sanitiser (when needed)**: 70% alcohol or hydrogen peroxide (never mix products).

## Surfaces
- Avoid vinegar on **stone** and **unfinished wood**.
- Microfiber towels for glass; cotton terry for counters.
