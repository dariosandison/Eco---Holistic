# Eco Kids’ Lunch Boxes

Tough, safe, easy to clean.

## What to Look For
- **Stainless steel** or **BPA‑free** compartments.
- **Leak‑proof** silicone lids for wet foods.
- **Machine‑washable** insulated bag.

## Packing Tips
- Include **protein + veg + fruit + carb**.
- Freeze yogurt tubes or use **ice packs** in summer.
