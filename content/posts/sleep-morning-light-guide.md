# The 10-minute rule
- Get outside within an hour of waking for 5–10 minutes (more if overcast).  
- Save sunglasses for later unless you’re sensitive.  
- Pair with **[Blue-Light Tools](/guides/best-blue-light-tools)** after sunset.
