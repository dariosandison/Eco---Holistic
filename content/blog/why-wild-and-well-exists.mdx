Wild & Well exists because most “wellness” content does two unhelpful things:

1) it makes you feel like you need to replace everything, and  
2) it hides the trade‑offs (ongoing costs, maintenance, real‑world fit).

I wanted a UK‑focused site that does the opposite: **one change at a time**, in plain English, with conservative claims.

> If you’re new here: [Start here →](/start-here)

## What we’re trying to solve
- **Decision fatigue:** too many options, too much marketing.
- **Hidden costs:** filters, refills, cartridges, returns.
- **Unrealistic expectations:** products can help — but routines usually matter more.

## How we choose what we recommend
We use a consistent rubric:
- **Fit for normal homes:** size, noise, usability, cleaning.
- **Total cost:** not just the device — replacements matter.
- **Availability:** UK stock and support.
- **Clear claims:** we prefer measurable specs and transparent brands.

We don’t accept paid placements in “best” lists.
