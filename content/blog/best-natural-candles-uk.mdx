    Natural Candles (UK): Buy Smarter, Burn Cleaner doesn’t have to be complicated. Below we explain what really matters, how to avoid common mistakes, and how to choose options that fit your budget and home.

### Why this matters
Good choices here affect health, comfort, and waste. The wrong pick costs more over time or simply goes unused. We focus on the few specs and habits that deliver outsized results.

**Key spec:** composition (wax, wick, fragrance type) and ventilation. Prefer cotton/paper wicks; burn for short sessions and ventilate after.

**Common mistakes:** burning too long, poor trimming, or using in unventilated small rooms.

### How to choose
- **Define the job**: room size, frequency of use, who’s using it.
- **Prioritise the spec that actually changes outcomes** (we call that out below for this category).
- **Total cost over 2–3 years**: filters/refills/consumables and expected lifespan.
- **Noise & ease**: if it’s annoying, people won’t use it.

### Setup & use
