If you struggle to fall asleep or feel “tired but wired”, caffeine timing is a common lever.

## A simple rule
Try an **8–10 hour** buffer between your last caffeine and bedtime.

## Adjust gradually
Move your last caffeine earlier by **60 minutes** every 3–4 days.
