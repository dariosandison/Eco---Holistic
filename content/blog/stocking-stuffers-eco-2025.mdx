- Spices, tea, chocolate with compostable wraps.  
- Small repair kits, quality socks, travel cutlery.  
- Solid shampoo/soap in a tin.  
- Refill vouchers for coffee or cleaning.

**Avoid:** novelty gadgets that break by February.
