# Reusables That Actually Save Money

Start with the items you use daily.

## Quick Wins
- **Bottle, mug, cutlery** set.
- **Microfiber** or **cotton** cloths for cleaning.
- **Glass** or **steel** food containers.

## Habits
- Pack a **go‑bag** near the door.
- Keep spares at work or in the car.
