## Experiences first
Workshops, repair cafés, meal kits, museum passes—memories beat objects.

## Consumables
Local coffee/tea, spice blends, beeswax wraps, bath salts with short ingredient lists.

## Useful tools
Quality kitchen shears, repair kits, refill vouchers, insulated bottles.

## Kids
Open‑ended toys, library memberships, craft materials.

## Wrap it right
Reused boxes, fabric furoshiki, paper tape, evergreen sprigs.
