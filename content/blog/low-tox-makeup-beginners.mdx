# Low‑Tox Makeup for Beginners

Start with skin comfort; expand slowly.

## TL;DR
- Switch **base** and **lip** first (highest contact).
- Choose **mineral pigments**, fragrance‑free where possible.
- Patch‑test new products.

## Build a Simple Kit
- Tinted mineral SPF or sheer foundation.
- Cream blush, brow gel, tubing mascara.
- Two hydrating lip colours.
