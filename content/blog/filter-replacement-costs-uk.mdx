Ongoing costs are the #1 reason people regret “wellness” purchases. The device is the easy part — **the replacement schedule is the real commitment**.

> **Quick links:** [Air Quality →](/topics/air-quality) • [Water →](/topics/water) • [Shortlists →](/shortlists)

## At a glance
- **Best habit:** check replacement prices *before* you buy.
- **Simple formula:** *(price per replacement) × (replacements per year)*.
- **Reality check:** “cheaper” devices can cost more over 12–24 months if replacements are pricey.

## The 3-step calculation (use this every time)
1) **Find the replacement you’ll actually buy**  
   Look for the exact cartridge/filter name, not a generic “fits most”.

2) **Work out frequency for *your* usage**  
   Manufacturers often publish a range (e.g., “every 2–6 months”).  
   If you don’t know, start conservative (more frequent) and adjust later.

3) **Calculate annual cost**
- **Annual cost** = *replacement price* × *replacements per year*
- **Monthly cost** = *annual cost* ÷ 12

<Note>
We avoid quoting “typical” prices because they vary a lot by brand and retailer. A quick check on the replacement page is more accurate than any estimate.
</Note>

## Examples by category (what to check)
### Air purifiers
- **Check:** HEPA filter price + availability in the UK
- **Frequency:** depends on run-time and household (pets, smoking, construction dust)
- **Hidden cost:** some brands bundle carbon + HEPA; others separate

- [Air purifiers for small flats (UK) →](/best-air-purifiers-small-flats-uk)

### Water filter jugs
- **Check:** cost per cartridge and how many are in a pack
- **Frequency:** often depends on litres filtered (not just months)
- **Hidden cost:** high-use households can burn through cartridges quickly

Helpful page:
- [Water filters (UK) →](/best-water-filters-uk)

### Under-sink systems
- **Check:** whether there are multiple stages (e.g., pre-filter + main filter)
- **Frequency:** some stages change more often than others
- **Hidden cost:** installation parts + occasional fittings

### Shower filters
- **Check:** cartridge price and whether it’s easy to replace
- **Frequency:** often depends on shower frequency and water hardness
- **Expectation:** treat as **comfort-first** (skin/hair feel), not a medical fix

Helpful page:
- [Shower filters (UK hard water) →](/best-shower-filters-uk-hard-water)

## Two rules that prevent buyer’s remorse
1) If you wouldn’t pay the replacement cost, **don’t buy the device**.  
2) If replacements are hard to find, **choose another brand** (maintenance will slip).
